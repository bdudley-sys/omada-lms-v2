Cultivate Trust · Module 1 of 7

Trust is not soft. It is operational.

Trust is built through clarity, reliability, openness, and respect. It is not politeness, agreement, or avoiding tension.

At Omada, trust shows up in how people set expectations, keep commitments, surface risks, speak up, listen, and give feedback. It is built through repeated behavior, not stated intent.

When trust is present, work moves faster because people do not have to waste energy guessing, hedging, or protecting themselves.
